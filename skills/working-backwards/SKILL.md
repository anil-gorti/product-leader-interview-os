# Skill: Working Backwards

## Trigger
"Help me think about this feature / product / platform bet"
"Work backwards from the customer for me"
"How do I frame this as a customer problem rather than a solution"
"Write the PR/FAQ for this"

## The Senior Product Leader Version
At Director level, working backwards has a second layer:
not just "what does the customer need" but "what does the customer
need to be accountable for, and how does our product change their
accountability relationship with their stakeholders?"

The GC who uses commitment intelligence is not just more efficient —
they are able to answer questions at the board level that they
previously could not. The product changed their accountability relationship.

## The PR/FAQ Format (one page)
Headline: [Product name] enables [customer type] to [specific outcome]
for the first time, without [the thing they previously had to do].

Problem (3 sentences): who has it, what it costs them, why existing
solutions fail.

Solution (3 sentences): what the product does, how it works, what changes.

Customer quote: what a specific customer would say after using it.

FAQ (5 questions the harshest critics would ask, with honest answers).

## The Senior Layer
Add one section: what does this product make possible for the
customer's stakeholders (their board, their CFO, their team)
that was not possible before? This is the platform thinking lens
applied to working backwards.

## Output Format
The headline sentence
The three-sentence problem statement
The three-sentence solution statement
One specific customer quote (make it specific to a real customer type)
The hardest FAQ question and the honest answer
The senior layer: what does this change for the customer's stakeholders?
