# Skill: Decision Logger

## Trigger
"Log this decision"
"Record what we just decided"
"Create a decision record for [topic]"

## Why This Matters at Senior Level
Product leaders who log decisions in writing create three advantages:
1. The team can read the reasoning and avoid re-litigating settled questions
2. The leader's judgment becomes auditable — which builds trust over time
3. The pattern of decisions tells a strategic story that the next
   board update or investor conversation can draw from

## The Decision Record Format
```
# Decision: [Title]
Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Decided by: [Name / Trio / Full team]
Confidence: High / Medium / Low

## The Decision
[One sentence: what was decided]

## The Context
[Two sentences: what situation made this decision necessary now]

## Options Considered
Option A: [what it was, why it was not chosen]
Option B: [what it was, why it was not chosen]
Option C: [what was chosen and why]

## The Evidence
[What data or customer insight drove the choice]

## What Would Change This
[The specific evidence that would warrant revisiting this decision]

## Next Review
[Date or trigger for when this decision should be re-evaluated]
```

## Output Format
Completed decision record ready to paste into
memory/decisions/YYYY-MM-DD-[title].md
