# Skill: Interview Debrief

## Trigger
"Debrief that session"
"What landed and what did not"
"Log this practice"
"How did I do"

## Process
1. Identify what was asked and what was answered
2. Score each answer on the senior standard:
   - Was it specific? (name, number, decision, outcome)
   - Was the transfer to the target company explicit?
   - Was the capital / team / platform framing present?
   - Would a harder interviewer have accepted it?
3. Identify the single most important fix before the real conversation
4. Log to memory/interview-sessions/YYYY-MM-DD-[interviewer].md

## The Senior Specificity Test
For each answer in the session, ask:
"Could any senior PM anywhere have given this answer?"
If yes — the answer is not ready. Push to make it only answerable
by this person, with this career, for this role.

## Output Format
**Overall**: [1-2 sentence honest read on the session quality]

**What landed**:
- [Specific moment, specific answer, why it worked]

**What was generic**:
- [Specific answer that drifted, the specific fix]

**The capital / team / platform framing**:
- Was it present? Where was it missing?

**The SpotDraft / target company transfer**:
- Was the bridge to this company's specific context explicit?
- Where could it have been stronger?

**The one thing to change before the real conversation**:
[Single most important fix — specific and actionable]

**Memory log entry**: [Ready to write — paste below]
